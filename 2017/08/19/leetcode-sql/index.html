  • t1 in the beginning: (t1.id - t2.id = 1 and t1.id - t3.id = 2 and t2.id - t3.id =1) – t1, t2, t3
  • t1 in the middle: (t2.id - t1.id = 1 and t2.id - t3.id = 2 and t1.id - t3.id =1) – t2, t1, t3
